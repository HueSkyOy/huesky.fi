Services

A perspective you can't get from the ground

An aerial shot shows a site's scale, location and surroundings at a glance – which is why it works so well for real estate, construction sites, events and the opening shots of marketing videos.

What I do

  • Aerial photos and videos for marketing
  • Real estate and area photography
  • Construction site and project documentation (e.g. progress shots at agreed intervals)
  • Aerial coverage of events

Technology and permits

The DJI Air 3S shoots 4K video and sharp stills even in low light. All flights comply with EU drone regulations – as a registered operator I handle the required notifications and airspace checks for you.

Tip

Combine aerials with a video or photo shoot on the same visit – two perspectives for the price of one trip.
