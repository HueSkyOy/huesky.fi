Services

Videos that tell your story and deliver results

A good corporate video doesn't just look good – it answers the viewer's question and drives action. I handle everything from idea to published video: script, filming, sound, editing, color grading and versions for each channel.

What I do

  • Company and service presentation videos
  • Customer testimonials and interviews
  • Event coverage and aftermovies
  • Social media videos and short ad formats (vertical and horizontal)

Technology

Filmed on the Canon R5 Mark II – with DJI Air 3S aerials in the same production when needed. Post-production in DaVinci Resolve Studio: professional editing, color grading and audio under one roof.

How it works

Brief and script → shoot day(s) → edit for your comments → final polish and delivery in the agreed formats.
