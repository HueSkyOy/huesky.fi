Services

Your event online – professionally and reliably

A stream succeeds when nobody thinks about the technology. I handle the broadcast from planning to teardown: camera angles, audio, graphics, presentation overlays and backups.

What I stream

  • Seminars and conferences
  • Hybrid events (on-site + remote)
  • Sports events
  • Concerts and cultural events
  • Internal company broadcasts and trainings

Technology

Multi-camera production with the ATEM Mini Pro switcher and OBS, running on a MacBook Pro M4 Max. Broadcast to YouTube, Vimeo, Teams or your platform of choice – including restricted audiences when needed. For larger productions I rent additional cameras and audio gear.

Bonus

The recording is yours to keep, and I can cut a highlight reel from it for later use.
